Data source

Data is fetched live from the Harvest Finance plasma-vault subgraph via /8453 (Base). TVL, APY, and share price come from plasmaVaultHistories, paginated by timestamp. The holders list and per-day snapshot are derived from userTransactions (deposit and withdraw events). Share-to-share ERC-20 transfers between users are not tracked here, so the holder count reflects addresses that have interacted with the vault directly.
